Privacy Policy · Last updated May 21, 2026

Summary: We do not collect, sell, or share your personal data. Everything you create in the app stays on your device. We do not use advertising, analytics trackers, or third-party data collection services.

This Privacy Policy describes how Proverbs 31: Daily Organizer Pro ("we", "our", or "the app"), published by Christian App Empire LLC, handles information when you use our mobile application on iOS and Android.

We do not collect your data

We do not operate servers that receive your personal information. We do not create user accounts, require sign-in, or transmit your tasks, habits, journal entries, themes, or settings to Christian App Empire or any third party.

  • No advertising networks or ad identifiers
  • No analytics or tracking SDKs
  • No cloud sync or backup to our servers
  • No sale or sharing of personal information

Information stored on your device

The app stores the content you create locally on your phone or tablet, including:

  • Tasks, habits, and journal entries
  • App preferences (themes, reminders, celebrations, Sabbath Pause)
  • Local notification schedules for task reminders

This data remains on your device until you delete it in the app, clear app storage, or uninstall the app. We cannot access or recover this data because it never leaves your device.

Notifications

If you enable task reminders, the app requests permission to send local notifications scheduled on your device. Reminder content is not sent to our servers. You can turn reminders off in the app Settings or in your device system settings at any time.

Purchases

Proverbs 31: Daily Organizer Pro is a paid app. Purchases and payment processing are handled entirely by the Apple App Store or Google Play Store. We do not receive or store your payment card details. Any purchase history is managed by the store according to its own privacy policy.

App store and device services

Apple and Google may collect limited diagnostic or crash information according to your device and store settings. That collection is governed by Apple or Google, not by us. The app does not add additional tracking beyond what those platforms provide by default.

Contact us

If you email us for support, we will use your message and email address only to respond to your request. We do not add support contacts to marketing lists.

For privacy questions, contact: ChristianAppEmpire@gmail.com

Children's privacy

The app is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Because we do not collect personal data through the app, no child-specific data is transmitted to us.

Your choices

  • Delete app content at any time within the app
  • Disable notifications in app Settings or system Settings
  • Uninstall the app to remove all locally stored data
  • Contact us to ask questions about this policy

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top will change when we do. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.
