Privacy Policy · Last updated May 20, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Proverbs 31 Organizer ("we", "our", or "the app") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile application on iOS and Android.

Information we collect

  • Information you provide: tasks, habits, journal entries, and settings you create in the app. This data is stored on your device unless you enable cloud sync in a future version.
  • Device identifiers: on iOS, with your permission via App Tracking Transparency, we may use your device advertising identifier to deliver ads through Google AdMob.
  • Usage and diagnostics: crash logs and performance data collected by app stores or analytics providers when enabled.

How we use information

  • Provide and improve app features (tasks, habits, journal, reminders)
  • Send local notifications when you enable reminders
  • Display advertisements through Google AdMob in the free version
  • Process in-app purchases for the Pro version

Advertising (AdMob)

The free version shows ads served by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and use data according to Google's Privacy Policy. You can limit ad tracking in your device Settings and through the App Tracking Transparency prompt on iOS.

Notifications

If you enable task reminders, the app requests permission to send local notifications. You can turn reminders off in Settings at any time.

In-app purchases

Pro purchases are processed by Apple App Store or Google Play. We do not receive your full payment card details. Purchase history is managed by the store.

Data retention

Content you create remains on your device until you delete it or uninstall the app. Ad and analytics partners retain data according to their own policies.

Children's privacy

The app is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your choices

  • Deny or limit tracking via iOS App Tracking Transparency
  • Disable notifications in system Settings
  • Upgrade to Pro to remove ads
  • Contact us to request deletion of support correspondence

Contact

For privacy questions, contact: support@proverbs31app.com

Changes

We may update this policy. The "Last updated" date at the top will change when we do. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.
